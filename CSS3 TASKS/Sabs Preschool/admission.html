ADMISSION

Procedure of Admission and Classes

For the registrations of the new academic session August 2016

Steps for the Admission:

  1. Registration forms
  2. Aptitude test
  3. Interview with the Principal
  4. Admission Order on the basis of the test and interview

Requirements:

  1. Birth Certificate
    • Please bring along the original birth certificate (this will be returned)
    • You are required to submit a photocopy of the same.
  2. Two passport sized photographs
  3. Progress report of the previous school (if any)
  4. Leaving Certificate of the previous schoo

Age Eligibility:

We offer classes for children 14 months through 5 years old. Each class is age appropriate and targeted towards learning.

Toddlers

Children who are 14 months and walking by August 1st may start this class in September. Toddlers will learn by experiencing the environment through their senses, by physically moving and through social interaction.

Climbers

Children who are 2 years old by August 1st start this class in September. Climbers will learn through experiences which emphasize language, activity and movement.

Runners

Children who are 3 years old and toilet trained by August 1st may start this class in September. Runners expand their knowledge through a variety of experiences. While building on previously learned material, they will enjoy more small motor activities. Child-initiated, teacher supported play gives the children experience in problem-solving.

Pre-K

Children who are 4 years old by August 1st start this class in September. The Pre-K children are exposed to experiences which combine ideas into more complex relations. Children will enjoy both fine and gross motor play. This class provides school readiness skills in preparation for kindergarten.
