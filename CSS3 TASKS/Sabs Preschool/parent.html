PARENTS CORNER

Be Online & Keep Getting Update of your Child Performance in School

The purpose of Parents Corner is to foster parents' interest in the school, and to promote cooperation, friendship and unity amongst the students, faculty and parents. Parents are expected to be actively involved in school projects and extra-curricular activities, and provide assistance in raising funds for various co-curricular initiatives. By using this facility, parents can keep track of their child's academic record, attendance and communicate with class teacher.
