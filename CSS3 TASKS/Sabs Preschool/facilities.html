FACILITIES

Spacious Classrooms

Spacious classrooms provide our students a conducive environment for learning. Classrooms have ample space for students to enjoy and utilize the knowledge that they have attained.

Swimming Pool

Swimming pools are designed for use by children and infants. Hence we have safety as paramount importance from the very beginning.

Audio Visual Room

As audio and visual aids is mandatory for learning, we have an exclusive Audio/ Visual room. The tiny tots watch entertaining and educative videos.

Extra Curricular Activities

Children are provided with opportunity to learn yoga, karate, dance, theatre, art and craft, sports and swimming as part of the curriculum of the school.

Music Lesson

Music lesson is a fun, playful early childhood music program that focuses on whole child development for babies, toddlers and preschoolers.

Excursion

Field Trips are organized every term. The children are taken to places which are close to Pondicherry for educational tours.

Cafeteria

The school has a cafeteria serving snacks and lunch. The importance of healthy dietary habit is stressed, but it cannot replace homemade, nutritious food.

Library

The policy of our library is to encourage the children to read for information and research and not only for recreation. Many library activities are conducted to make sure the children know the value of reading.

Bus Service

Well maintained and comfortable buses are equipped with support staff who have mobile phones which help monitor each route. Our drivers ensure a safe journey.

Playground

The school also has playground for your child's better physiacal and mental fitness. Playground equipment is designed expecially for preschool children.
